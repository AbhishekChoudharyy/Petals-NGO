Our Services

We envision a community where everyone has access to good quality, nutritious food, understands the consequences of hunger and poor nutrition and is committed to creating a stronger, healthier community.

Food Drives

We conduct weekly drives to provide food to the needy. Donating to the needy is a great way to improve the conditions in your neighborhood or community. Donating food to the worthy people or organizations helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness, and trust among residents.

Animal Care

We help animals who need assistance. Our mission is to ensure every creature receives proper care and attention. We dedicate ourselves to rescuing and nurturing animals in distress. Through our programs, we work tirelessly to improve animal welfare. We believe every animal deserves love and compassion.

Education

We believe every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive. Through quality education, mentorship, and resources, we empower young minds to break barriers, dream big, and shape a better future for themselves and their communities.

Old Ages

We are dedicated to serving our elderly community with unwavering commitment and compassion. Our mission focuses on providing comprehensive care that encompasses both physical and emotional well-being. Through daily support and meaningful interactions, we strive to create an environment where seniors can thrive and feel valued.

About Us

❖ PETALS NGO is a non-profit organization that was started with the mission of helping the poor and animals. We conduct weekly drives to provide food to the needy and also help animals in need.

❖ We believe in the dignity of all living beings and strive to provide care and support to animals in need. We also organize events to raise awareness about the issues of hunger and animal welfare.

❖ We provide health care services to the needy. We believe in a world where no child goes to bed hungry and where all living beings are treated with respect and care.

My Journey with Petals Foundation

I am Shikhar Srivastava, founder of Petals Foundation. My journey began after passing 12th when I was preparing for NEET.

Every day, on my way to coaching, I saw stray animals struggling for food and elderly women begging on the streets. It hurt me deeply, and I initially tried to help by donating to organizations.

However, I soon realized we cannot always rely on others to fulfill our duty as humans. If real change was needed, I had to take action myself. That's when I decided to start Petals Foundation. It began in Lucknow, then expanded to Raebareli, Delhi, Mumbai & over 32 cities across India. The journey was full of struggles—financial challenges, societal resistance, and countless obstacles—but I never looked back.

With time, we grew pan-India and expanded overseas to Bali (Indonesia), San Diego (USA), and Lahore (Pakistan). What started as a small effort became a movement.

My mission is clear: no elderly woman should have to beg, no stray animal should suffer for food, and no slum child should lack education. I know the road ahead is tough, but I am determined to keep going until we reach that goal.

This is just the beginning.

Our Guiding Principles

We believe in engaging the community and involving those we serve in finding solutions to eliminate hunger and food insecurity. We believe the root causes of hunger must be addressed to realize our long-term goal of eliminating hunger. We believe collaboration and partnerships - internally and externally - are essential to fulfilling our mission. We recognize the dignity of all people and believe food is an essential right. We believe hunger can be eliminated.

Teamwork

We will foster cooperation and a sense of empowerment amongst our diverse staff, volunteers, and community partners.

Respect

We will treat all of our relationships with respect.

Innovation

We will continually seek to provide excellent service through the development of new and improved methods to reduce hunger.

Empathy, Passion, Empowerment

We encourage and support the best in ourselves and those we serve and provide opportunities for everyone to participate in fulfilling our mission.
